About PCH

Padma Convention Hall (PCH) is one of the ORIGINAL and LEADING convention centers in Bangladesh. It was established in 2003 with the goal of providing the highest standard of services in the Hospitality Industry.

Padma Convention Hall offers Luxurious Banquet Halls, Business Conference Rooms, Business Centre, all of which that are equipped with a modern hygienic Kitchen, car parking facilities and a fantastic and cozy location in the central business district of Gulshan, Dhaka.

PCH is managed by a well-trained term of members who are all placed at your kind service with an integrated approach’ from all corners.

Our establishment in perfect for both corporate and Social Events, Such events include, Annual General Meeting, Business exhibitions, Seminars, Diplomatic events, Wedding Events, Birthday Parties, Get Together, Thanks Giving Dinner, Convocation Ceremony or any other Indoor Events; all of which that warrant large Ball Room(s)

PCH is responsible for outside catering for both Corporate and Social events. A complete outdoor Catering Service for large/small functions in and around Dhaka City is available.
